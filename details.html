Contest Winner!

Congratulations to Zeus, our contest winner!

Zeus is the winner of our cutest pet contest! He is a black axolotl who has been with us for just under 2 years.

As an axolotl, Zeus enjoys swimming around in his tank and playing with some of his other tank mates. He loves attention from our volunteers, and even tries to eat their fingers when we feed him! We hope that Zeus will come to find a forever home soon. If you wish to adopt him, please give us a call.
